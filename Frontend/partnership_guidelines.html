Partnership Guidelines

Building a trusted community of professionals together.

Welcome to the ServiceBazaar Partner Network

By joining ServiceBazaar, you're not just getting a new source of customers; you're becoming part of a community dedicated to quality, reliability, and professionalism. These guidelines are designed to ensure a fair, safe, and successful environment for both our partners and customers.

Our Core Principles

The foundation of a successful partnership.

Commitment to Quality

Deliver high-quality workmanship on every job. Your expertise is your reputation, and it's the core of our platform's promise to customers.

Professional Conduct

Maintain a professional and respectful attitude at all times. This includes timely communication, appropriate attire, and courteous interaction with clients.

Safety and Trust

Adhere to all safety standards relevant to your trade. Be honest and transparent with customers about the work required and the pricing.

Code of Conduct

Clear expectations for every interaction.

You Are Expected To:

  • Respond to booking requests promptly.
  • Arrive on time for scheduled appointments.
  • Provide clear and upfront pricing for your services.
  • Complete the agreed-upon work to a high standard.
  • Leave the customer's property clean and tidy.

The Following Are Prohibited:

  • Taking payments or jobs "off-platform" for bookings initiated through ServiceBazaar.
  • Misrepresenting your skills, qualifications, or services.
  • Any form of harassment, discrimination, or unsafe behavior.
  • Cancelling confirmed jobs without a valid, urgent reason.

Violation of these guidelines may result in a temporary or permanent suspension of your account.
